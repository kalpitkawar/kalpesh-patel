        FAQ & Help

        How do I add a demat account?
        Go to your profile, scroll to "My Demat Accounts", fill the form, and click Add.
        How do I track my IPO applications?
        Use the "My IPO Applications" section to log and view your IPO applications and status.
        How do I check IPO allotment for all my accounts?
        Click the "Check All Allotments" button in the Demat Accounts section.
        How do I get IPO notifications?
        Enable email alerts in your profile and check the Notifications section for updates.
        How do I export my data?
        Use the Export buttons above to download your demat or IPO application data as CSV.
